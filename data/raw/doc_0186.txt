Unsupervised learning finds patterns in unlabeled data. Clustering groups similar instances together. K-means partitions data into k clusters based on feature similarity. Hierarchical clustering creates a tree of clusters. Principal Component Analysis reduces dimensionality while preserving variance. Anomaly detection identifies unusual patterns. This variant explores data_science from a practical perspective with real-world examples. Understanding these concepts is essential for building robust and scalable systems.
