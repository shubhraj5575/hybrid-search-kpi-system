Encryption protects data confidentiality. Symmetric encryption uses the same key for encryption and decryption. Asymmetric encryption uses public and private key pairs. TLS/SSL secures data in transit. At-rest encryption protects stored data. Key management is critical for maintaining security. Perfect forward secrecy limits damage from key compromise. This variant explores security from a practical perspective with real-world examples. Understanding these concepts is essential for building robust and scalable systems.
